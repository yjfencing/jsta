Comprehensive Personal Tax Filing

We offer complete personal tax preparation for individuals at every stage of life. Whether you’re filing a straightforward return or have complex income sources, we take care of the details so you can file with confidence. Our team reviews each return for eligible credits, deductions, and benefits to help you minimize your tax liability and maximize your refund.

We also assist with CRA reviews, notices of assessment, and any questions or audits you may receive. Our goal is to provide you with peace of mind, knowing your taxes are handled accurately and professionally.

Common Situations We Assist With

We support all types of personal tax scenarios, from simple to highly complex. Whether you're an employee, student, retiree, or business owner, our services are designed to fit your unique financial situation.

  • Employment income (T4) and standard personal tax returns
  • Self-employed and contractor returns (T2125)
  • Rental property income and expenses
  • Investment income, dividends, and capital gains
  • Tuition, education credits, and student returns
  • Retirement income, pensions, CPP/OAS, RRSP withdrawals
  • Newcomer and first-time filer assistance
