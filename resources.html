Frequently Asked Questions

General Tax Questions

  • When are tax returns due?
    Most personal tax returns are due April 30th. Self-employed individuals have until June 15th, but payments are still due April 30th.
  • What documents should I bring for my tax filing?
    T-slips, receipts, income summaries, RRSP contributions, tuition forms, medical receipts, and business expense records if applicable.
  • Do you accept new clients?
    Yes — simply visit our Contact page to get started.

Personal Tax Questions

  • Can I claim home office expenses?
    This depends on employment status and CRA eligibility requirements. Contact us for personalized guidance.
  • What deductions am I eligible for?
    Credits and deductions vary by situation — tuition, RRSP, moving expenses, medical, childcare, etc.

Corporate Tax Questions

  • Do you file GST/HST returns?
    Yes — monthly, quarterly, and annual filings.
  • Can you assist with bookkeeping?
    Absolutely. We offer complete bookkeeping, payroll, and financial reporting.
